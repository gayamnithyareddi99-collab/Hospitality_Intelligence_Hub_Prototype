The Attribution Gap

UK Restaurants Are Flying Blind

Over 72% of diners discover restaurants via social media, yet most operators cannot identify which post or creator filled a table — costing the UK £3.2B annually.

02

Operational Silos

Social platforms, reservation systems, and POS tools remain disconnected — with no unified view of guest behavior or spend causality.

03

No-Show Epidemic

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Answers to common questions about the platform, attribution methodology, GDPR compliance, pricing, and pilot programme details.

The attribution gap is the inability of restaurant operators to connect a specific social media post, creator, or campaign to an actual seated cover and confirmed spend at their venue. Over 72% of UK diners discover restaurants via social, yet only 8% of restaurants have any system to track this. We close this gap with our closed-loop AI engine.
All data collection and processing is fully GDPR-compliant under UK GDPR Article 6 (legitimate interests and consent). Guest data is anonymized before attribution analysis. The Community Intelligence Network (CIN) uses federated learning — meaning raw data never leaves the venue's environment. We are ICO-registered and provide a full data processing agreement (DPA) with every subscription.
For most venues, API integration with your POS and reservation system takes 3–5 business days. Social platform connections (TikTok, Instagram, Google Maps) activate within 24 hours. Full onboarding including staff training is typically complete within 7–10 days. Enterprise multi-site setup takes 2–4 weeks. Our pilot programme includes dedicated implementation support.
The 8-week pilot includes full platform access (Professional tier features), free POS and social integration, dedicated onboarding support, weekly attribution reports, and a final ROI analysis document. There is no payment required during the pilot period and no obligation to subscribe at the end. We aim to demonstrate measurable revenue improvement within 23 days.
We integrate natively with Zonal POS, Lightspeed, Square, and TouchBistro. For reservations: OpenTable, SevenRooms, ResDiary, and Resy. Social platforms: TikTok Business API, Instagram Graph API, and Google My Business. Accounting: Xero and Sage. If your current system is not listed, contact us — custom API integrations are available as part of Enterprise onboarding.
Our pilot data shows operators receive their first meaningful attribution insights within 23 days on average. Revenue impact (22% average lift) becomes measurable within 6–8 weeks as the ML model learns your specific venue patterns. COCP operational alerts begin firing within the first week of social data ingestion — often preventing a service crisis in the very first month.
